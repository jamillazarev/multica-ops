(PLAYBOOKS → "Discover the process, then the tools"). Research how the craft actually does
this work well (not from memory), draft the steps each with a one-line why, show the owner to
cut/add/reorder, then search a skill/MCP/tool **per step by its function**, broadening on an
empty result. Name any step with no tool as a gap. Record the chosen process in `docs/`.
