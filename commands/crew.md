description: Crew mode — executors and gates with no conductor; the owner is the product manager. Default after an import.
---

Load and follow the **multica-ops** skill (SKILL.md), executing its **crew mode** flow. The
owner assigns work directly and decides what is next; there is no conductor, no discovery
and no roadmap ceremony. Everything else still applies — the guide, review gates, limits
and recovery, permission rules, dated work. If the owner starts asking *what* should be
next rather than telling, offer a conductor once, with the reason.
